Aloe Vera

Scientific Name: Aloe barbadensis miller

Benifits: acne, lichen planus (a very itchy rash on the skin or in the mouth), oral submucous fibrosis, burning mouth syndrome, burns, and radiation-induced skin toxicity

The aloe vera grows only under the sun with well-drained dry or moist soil. Although the plant tastes like a turd, it's still edible. The sap from aloe vera is extremely useful to

speed up the healing & reducing the risk of infections for :

  • wounds
  • cuts
  • burns
  • reducing inflammation

Apart from its external use on the skin, aloe vera is also taken internally in the treatment of :

  • ulcerative colitis (drinking aloe vera juice)
  • chronic constipation
  • poor appetite
  • digestive problems
