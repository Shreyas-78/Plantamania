Mango Tree

Scientific Name: Mangifera indica

Benifits: The juice is restorative tonic and used in heat stroke. The seeds are used in asthma and as an astringent

The leaves, bark, and fruit of the mango tree are used in traditional medicine to treat a variety of conditions, including skin disorders, fever, and digestive problems

Mango tree is used for:

  • For hair issues
  • Promotes healthy skin
  • Controls blood pressure
  • Combats restlessness
  • Controls blood sugar level
  • Cures dysentery
  • Cures stomach ulcers and hiccups
  • Treats kidney and gallstones
