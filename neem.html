Neem Tree

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

Benifits: treatment of inflammation, infections, fever, skin diseases and dental disorders

The leaves, seeds, and bark of the neem tree are used in traditional medicine to treat a wide range of ailments, including fever, infections, and skin disorders

Neem trees are used for:

  • Promote hair health
  • Boost dental and oral health
  • May aid liver and kidney health
  • May improve skin health
  • Ulcer and wound healing
  • Helps in destroing harmful bacteria
  • Oral Health
